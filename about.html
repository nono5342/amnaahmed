who am i?

my name is amna ahmed, and this is my little corner on the internet.

i am a sudanese computer scientist in the making, and an enthusiast for everything front-end. i enjoy design, programming, and writing, and this website is a creative space where i combine all three.

here, you can find my projects, writing, links to my social media, and random little bits of me.

what do i do?

i learn stuff, make stuff, and have a great deal of fun while at it.

i'm a self-taught front end web developer, currently experimenting with ux/ui design.

as of 2022, i am a sophomore at ain shams university in egypt, studying at the faculty of computer and information sciences. i'm really enjoying university, and have met a lot of awesome people here (if you're here and you know me from uni, you're awesome!).
